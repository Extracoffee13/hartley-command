👑 The Archetypes
Joseph · The Pit Before Egypt
Thrown in a pit at 17. Sold into slavery. Falsely accused. Imprisoned. Every step looked like the dream dying. It was the dream being built. The compression of 13 years produced the authority to run an empire at 30. No pit, no palace. No preparation, no position.
David · The Cave Before the Crown
Anointed king as a shepherd boy. Spent years in caves before sitting on the throne — hunted, misunderstood, isolated. The cave was his executive training program. It produced a king who knew what it cost. The crown sits differently when you earned it in the dark.
